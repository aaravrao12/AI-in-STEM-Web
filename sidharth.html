Sidharth Narayanan

Chapter President

Sidharth Narayanan leads the Coppell chapter of AI in STEM with a focus on innovation, inclusion, and collaboration. With hands-on experience in programming and robotics, he is dedicated to empowering students through accessible AI education and real-world problem-solving opportunities.

About Sidharth

Sidharth Narayanan is a student at Coppell High School and the first Chapter President of AI in STEM Coppell. He discovered his passion for STEM through school courses and hands-on experience on his school’s tech team, where he built coding projects and contributed to real-world solutions. His drive to create, learn, and share has led him to become a leader in both the classroom and his community.

Leadership

President of AI in STEM Coppell Chapter

Technical Skills

Experienced in Java, JavaScript, and Python; web and robotics software developer

Collaboration

Team-focused developer in STEM-For-Others and robotics competitions

As a developer and leader, Sidharth has worked on a range of STEM projects, including web applications and robotics software. His goal is to take on technically challenging tasks while improving his problem-solving skills and contributing to meaningful impact through AI education.

Beyond academics, Sidharth enjoys building with code, exploring AI tools, and sharing knowledge with others. Through his work with AI in STEM, he hopes to inspire more students to explore how technology can be used to innovate and uplift communities.

Sidharth's Vision

"My goal is to build an inclusive space where students from all backgrounds can explore AI, solve problems, and develop the skills that will shape tomorrow’s world."
