About AI in Stem

We're a teen-led movement making AI fun, accessible, and hands-on for the next generation of creators, coders, and change-makers.

What We Do

We're AI in STEM, a student-led nonprofit on a mission to help every kid — no matter where they're from — learn, explore, and get excited about the world of AI and tech.

We started this in early 2025 from our school in Plano, Texas (shoutout to Jasper High!) because most AI education out there is expensive, boring, or made for adults. So we built something different — by students, for students.

Whether you're just getting started or already love coding, we've got you covered with:

  • Free tutorials and online courses

    that are easy to follow and designed for beginners

  • Cool projects

    that let you actually build stuff and see results

  • Fun challenges, clubs, and meetups

    to learn with others and build community

  • Teen mentors

    who are here to help you grow and succeed

We're all about making AI and STEM fun, free, and for everyone — no fancy background or expensive gear needed. Let's learn, build, and dream big together. 🚀

Why It Matters

The future is AI. But right now, most kids are stuck on the sidelines. Expensive courses, confusing tools, and limited access are keeping too many from unlocking their potential.

  • Every student deserves a chance to learn real-world tech, no matter where they live or what their background is.

  • Diversity in tech starts with access to education — early and often.

  • Learning AI isn't just coding — it's creativity, collaboration, and confidence.
